Professional Biography

I am a Computer Programming student at George Brown College with a strong interest in building practical, user-focused software. I enjoy working across the full stack — from designing clean interfaces to building reliable backend services and databases. Through coursework and team projects, I have gained hands-on experience with technologies such as JavaScript/Node.js, APIs, databases (SQL and NoSQL), version control with Git/GitHub, and deploying projects in a way that is easy for others to run and review.

My current goal is to start my career in software development in a role where I can keep improving my technical skills while contributing to real products. I value clear communication, consistent progress, and building systems that are stable, maintainable, and easy to understand.
